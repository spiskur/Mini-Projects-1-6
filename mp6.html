Sharon Piskur's Mini Project 6

Explanation of Editing:

For MP6, I used photo and video clips that I have taken so far this school year. Every year, I create a video of classroom memories and we view it at our fifth grade graduation celebration. I used the Photos app on Windows 10 to create the video. I found the program to be user friendly while in create mode, but less user friendly for converting to a smaller format. The effects I used were different types of headings leading into new topics, a comet that moves during the rocket launch video, and embedded video within the video itself. Some of the photos were cropped to bring out the subject of the photo. Three photos were edited in PhotoShop. My favorite feature of the Photos app is they have stock music that automatically fits to the length of the video. I also have the option to upload my own music instead. I will use the upload feature at the end of the year because we have "graduation" songs that are traditional for our ceremonies.
