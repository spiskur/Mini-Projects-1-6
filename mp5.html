Reflection:

Learning how to use Dreamweaver has been incredibly useful. It is very user friendly and takes a lot of the question out of coding puzzles. It is easy to have the tabs for each mini project open at the top of the page and be able to copy and paste into the pages while making adjustments. Even using the Quick Tag Editor is quick and easy. Creating a classroom website will be faster since adding, deleting, and updating the pages is simple. I am glad we learned to hand-code first. Learning the process of coding gave me an understanding and made Dreamweaver a luxury that will make our work more professional and creative.
